Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 February 2025

At pybanger, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website, make a purchase, or communicate with us.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Info: Name, email, Telegram ID (if shared), and UPI, Binance Pay ID, or USDT wallet address (for crypto payments)
  • Technical Info: Device type, IP address, browser info, and cookies
  • Transaction Details: Payment history, order info, and support communications

We do not collect or store any credit/debit card data. All crypto/UPI transactions are processed via secure external gateways.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and confirm your purchases
  • To deliver EA licenses, updates, or custom tools
  • To provide technical support and respond to inquiries
  • To send important updates or changes to our terms
  • To improve website performance and user experience

We do not sell or share your data with advertisers or third-party marketers.

3. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and tools like Google Analytics to:

  • Track traffic and user behavior
  • Enhance site performance
  • Analyze conversions and marketing effectiveness

You may disable cookies via your browser settings.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your information is securely stored using encrypted protocols. Access is restricted to authorized team members only. We host our services on secure, reputable platforms.

5. Communications and Opt-Outs

We may contact you via email or Telegram to share:

  • Order confirmations
  • Product or security updates
  • Support responses

To opt-out of marketing communications, email support@pybanger.com.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Your Rights (GDPR & Global)

You may request to:

  • Access or correct your personal data
  • Delete your information
  • Restrict processing or object to certain uses

Contact support@pybanger.com to make such requests.

8. Third-Party Tools

We use the following services, which have their own privacy policies:

  • Binance Pay / UPI for crypto transactions
  • Google Analytics for traffic analysis
  • Telegram for optional support chats

9. Changes to This Policy

We may occasionally update this privacy policy. Any updates will be posted here with a revised date.
